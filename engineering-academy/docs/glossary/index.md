# Glossary

This section is a lookup tool, not a narrative — use it when a chapter mentions a term you don't recognize, rather than reading it front-to-back.

## Available glossaries

| Glossary | Covers |
|---|---|
| [Domain & Codebase Terms](/glossary/domain-terms) | Dhandho-specific and general software terms used throughout the Academy |
| [India GST Terms](/glossary/india-gst-terms) | Tax and compliance vocabulary specific to India's GST regime |

## Why two separate glossaries

The GST domain has a large, India-specific vocabulary (IRN, EWB, HSN, GSTIN, CESS, ITC...) that's unfamiliar to most engineers regardless of experience level, and conflating it with general codebase terms (RLS, JWT, tenant, RPC) would make both harder to scan. If you're debugging a GST feature, go straight to [India GST Terms](/glossary/india-gst-terms); if you're new to the codebase generally, start with [Domain & Codebase Terms](/glossary/domain-terms).
